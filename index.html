01 ABOUT US

Built on trust.Driven by precision.

Established in June 2020 by Ayanda and Aphumelela Kanuka in the tightly knit community of Komani (Queenstown), Eastern Cape. What began as a local practice serving neighbours and small traders has grown into a trusted financial services partner for businesses across the province.

We exist to bring institutional-grade financial discipline to entrepreneurs who are building something real — without the jargon, without the condescension, and without the corporate distance.

"We don't just manage your books.We help you understand them."

— Ayanda Kanuka, Managing Director
